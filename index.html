    Common questions about Chicago districts

    How do I find out what ward I'm in?

    Search your address or click your location on the map above. The tool shows your Chicago ward and who represents it, along with every other civic district that covers that exact point.

    How many wards does Chicago have?

    Chicago has 50 wards, each represented by an alderperson on the City Council.

    What are my Chicago police district and beat?

    Chicago is divided into 22 police districts, each split into smaller beats. Select any address above to see the police district and beat that cover it, plus the nearest police station.

    What is a Chicago community area?

    Chicago has 77 official community areas — long-established neighborhood boundaries used for planning and statistics. The tool shows which community area contains any point you pick.

    Who represents me in Congress and in Springfield?

    Pick a location to see your U.S. House district, Illinois State Senate district, and Illinois State House district, along with the people currently holding those seats.

    Is this an official government tool?

    No. It draws on public data sources that disclaim legal precision, so always confirm district assignments and officeholders with the relevant government office before relying on them for anything official.

    About this data
